Mansion Exterior

Dracula sleeps peacefully.
It's a bit hard to believe he's a vampire when he looks almost human enough to be identical to the typical posh person.
The time is right, so you approach for an ambush.

Suddenly, Dracula's eyes flash open and is alerted of your presence.
"What in the- How did you get here?"
He opens a nearby drawer and surprisingly pulls out a gun. "It'd take a miracle if I ever see you again!"
While it definitely seems like Dracula, the fact he is standing in full sunlight with the curtains open triggers your fight or flight response.
