Mansion Exterior

It's October 31st and Dracula's Mansion has risen after a century of slumber!!!
...in the middle of Utica, New York. It has received some renovations throughout the years, no longer appearing as the castle as it once was.
Don't worry, you have brought a suspicious conch shell that you brought for twenty dollars.
You can play it to return back to the outside of the mansion.

Dracula is actually quite kind to any guests that come in. However, that doesn't matter.
You hate him! So you woke up extra early to be the first one there to get to the mansion!
Your objective is to find your way to his room and kill him. However, you have entered in completely unarmed. You need to find a way to also arm yourself before you actually arrive.
